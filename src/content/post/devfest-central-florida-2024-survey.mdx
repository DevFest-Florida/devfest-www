## **DevFest Central Florida: Your Feedback Matters!**

**Help Us Shape the Future of DevFest**

We hope you had an incredible time at DevFest Central Florida! Your participation and enthusiasm made this year's event a resounding success.
To ensure that future DevFests are even better, we're inviting you to share your thoughts and experiences through our **DevFest Central Florida Impact Survey**. Your feedback is invaluable in helping us identify areas for improvement and enhance your overall experience.

**By taking this brief survey, you'll contribute to:**

* **Improved guest experience:** Helping us tailor future events to your needs and preferences.
* **Enhanced event content:** Ensuring that the sessions and workshops align with your interests and career goals.
* **Strengthened community:** Fostering a more inclusive and supportive environment for developers.

**Take the Surveys Now:**
* [DevFest Florida Central Impact Survey](https://forms.gle/uiLhbQcpb1SQEiWd7) (local survey takes 5 minutes)
* [DevFest Survey from Google Developer Group office](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1OkOsO1OXFfeVKfUzGBdArLxJTdWIqrM1t45bugyELS0/viewform?edit_requested=true) (3 minutes)

**Join Our Discord Community**

Want to connect with other developers and stay involved in the Google Developer Group(GDG) of Central Florida community year-round? Join our Discord server for discussions, announcements, and more.  We hold monthly events to help empower you on your developer journey.
