## DevFest GemJam | October 25 - November 2

![GemJam Workshop](~/assets/images/cloudTrack.jpg)

Join us for **DevFest GemJam**, a flagship DevFest Florida event designed to spark innovation and collaboration. This week-long creative sprint culminates in an in-person Kickoff on **October 25th** and a Demo Day on **November 2** at the **Seminole State College Wayne M. Densch Partnership Center** in Sanford, FL.

Demo day for projects and pitches will be held at [Tech Hub Orlando](https://innovateorlando.io/tech-hub-orlando/)

This is a welcoming space for people of all backgrounds to come together, imagine, build, and share ideas for a better future using Generative AI. We emphasize collaboration, creativity, and community impact, approaching AI as a tool for augmentation, not replacement.

Whether you're a coder, designer, storyteller, or visionary, explore how to use AI responsibly and ethically to craft groundbreaking solutions with [Google's Gemini AI suite](https://aistudio.google.com/), [Google Cloud](https://cloud.google.com/free?utm_source=PMAX&utm_medium=display&utm_campaign=FY24-na-US-DR-pmax-1710134&utm_content=pmax&gclsrc=aw.ds&gad_source=1&gad_campaignid=22037004910&gclid=Cj0KCQjwrojHBhDdARIsAJdEJ_dg0ONPbnG-eEJBtLlYa3HVw10EfDIzMhIVUMFYtReZmv0XrpVZeKkaAsn1EALw_wcB&hl=en), and [Firebase Studio](https://firebase.studio/)
